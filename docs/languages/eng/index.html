What is Pasajero?

Pasajero is the premier digital platform for organizing and improving access to informal public transportation, starting with cities like Tijuana. This user-friendly, collaborative application enables passengers to easily discover nearby routes, identify vehicles by their unique colors and types, and receive timely arrival estimates. By allowing users to voluntarily register when they board, the platform continuously enhances the accuracy of its data.

What You Will Be Able to Do with Pasajero

Find nearby routes on an interactive map.

Identify vehicles by type and color.

Receive arrival estimates.

Register your trip with a single click when you board.
