1) Scope

This Policy applies to our website, early access signups, beta testing programs, and features that help you find nearby routes, identify vehicles by type/color, receive arrival estimates, and optionally register your trip when you board.

2) Data We Collect

Information you provide

  • Email (for news, launch updates, and beta invites)
  • Name
  • Colony/Area where you live (helps suggest nearby routes)
  • Beta interest (whether you’d like to participate in tests)
  • How you found us (referral source)
  • Preferences (e.g., nearby routes, real-time tracking, prices & schedules)
  • Trip registration (if you tap “I boarded,” we record that event)

Information collected automatically

  • Device & log data (IP, browser, OS, crash logs, diagnostics)
  • Usage data (pages, clicks, features used, timestamps)
  • Approx. location from IP; precise location only if you grant permission
  • Cookies & similar for essentials, analytics, and (if enabled) marketing

You can choose not to provide certain fields, but some features may not work.

3) How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve core features (route discovery, vehicle identification, ETAs)
  • Operate optional trip registration to enhance data accuracy
  • Send product news, launch updates, and beta invitations (unsubscribe anytime)
  • Run analytics to understand usage and improve performance and safety
  • Detect, prevent, and address security or abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

4) Legal Bases (EEA/UK)

  • Contract/Service – to provide requested features
  • Consent – for marketing emails, precise location, analytics/marketing cookies
  • Legitimate interests – to improve and secure our services
  • Legal obligation – when we must keep or share data with authorities

5) Sharing & Service Providers

We use vetted processors for hosting, analytics, email delivery, and support. They only process data under our instructions and appropriate safeguards. We may share data to comply with laws, enforce terms, or protect safety. We do not sell your personal information.

6) Data Retention

  • Account & contact data: kept while active and a reasonable period thereafter
  • Trip registration & telemetry: kept in identifiable form for a limited time, then aggregated or anonymized
  • Logs and diagnostics: typically 13 months (unless required longer for security/legal reasons)

7) Cookies & Similar Technologies

We use essential cookies (required), and—if you consent—analytics and marketing cookies.

Essential

Security, load balancing, core features.

Analytics (opt-in)

Understand usage and improve the app.

Marketing (opt-in)

Measure campaigns and promote features.

8) Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or download your data; to object or restrict processing; and to withdraw consent (e.g., marketing emails or cookies) at any time. You can unsubscribe from emails via the link in any message. You may also contact us to exercise rights or make a complaint with your local authority.

9) Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 16 (or as required by local law). If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

10) Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

11) International Data Transfers

If data is transferred across borders, we use appropriate safeguards (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses) as required by law.

12) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. We’ll change the “Effective” date above and, when appropriate, provide additional notice.
